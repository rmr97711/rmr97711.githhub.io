Chipper Jones

By Rylan Rigdon

Larry Wayne Jones Jr. , know to the baseball world as Chipper Jones, was a Major League Baseball player for the Atlanta Braves. This baseball hero was born on April 24, 1972 in Deland, Florida. He was a baseball prodigy from day one and was one of the best high school players growing up. As he is one of few individuals that are able to hit consistently from both sides of the plate. This was proven when the selected with the first overall pick in the 1990 draft by the Braves. He played left field and third base for the Braves from 1995 to 2012. He spent his entire professional baseball career with the Atlanta organization.
